FAQ

Built for recovery, not punishment.

Is this a habit tracker?

No. Habit tracking is one module. The core product is assessment, prescription, classified goal coaching, and review adjustment.

Can I use it before registering?

Yes. The assessment can be completed first. Registration saves the result and creates the first training plan.

Does Threadline diagnose medical or mental health conditions?

No. It avoids medical, addiction, and psychological diagnosis. It focuses on action planning and goal training.
